Sports Sandals

Sport sandals are great for hiking and extended wear times while being physically active. Sport sandals for men and women are more durable and generally more badass, which is something I like to look for in all of my footwear.

flip flops

The difference between Flip Flops and Sandals lies in their construction, Flip Flops are generally made of a soft and flat sole with a Y-shaped strap that are attached on the sole between the place for the big toe and the second toe to hold your feet back while Sandals are a flat or high heeled sole that has straps

Everyday sandals

Usually, people wear sandals in warmer climates or during warmer parts of the year in order to keep their feet cool and dry. The risk of developing athlete's foot is lower than with enclosed shoes, and the wearing of sandals may be part of the treatment regimen for such an infection.
